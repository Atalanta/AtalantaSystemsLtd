System Administration

When you need external sysadmin help

  • Not all companies have in-house system administration resources. It can be more cost-effective to outsource, paying only as and when you need help. We can provide hourly or daily consultancy rates and staff to meet your needs.
  • Many IT projects require a degree of specialism that even the best system administrators may not have. Few companies can afford to employ expertise at this level and instead choose to contract experts to assist with implementation of a project. Whatever area of expertise you need augmented, speak to us - we can put a team together for you.
  • Sometimes seemingly intractable problems present themselves and you need to call in short-term specialist help. Whatever the problem, speak to us and we can provide assistance.

Why use Atalanta

Whatever technology your business is using, we can help you.
