Agile Infrastructure Consultancy

Atalanta Systems is a UK-based infrastructure consultancy, almost 5 years old, with a varied portfolio of small and large clients.

What we do

We improve profitability for e-businesses and technology-driven start-ups for whom their technical infrastructure is mission-critical, by optimising their infrastructure and dev-to-ops processes. We help keep the infrastructure running smoothly, delivering value in a way which allows the business to concentrate on the business rather than the plumbing.

How we do it

By reducing waste in the value chain from software development to production.

By architecting and implementing highly available, resilient and secure infrastructures, delivered so as to minimise operational management cost.

By designing bespoke solutions using the best of the current tools and methodologies, including some of our own in-house tools.

With best-value resourcing and getting the best out of our people via Agile and Devops processes and philosophies.

By developing less-explored technology niches.

Why we do it well

We have been leading the way in agile infrastructure development for four years. Our principal consultant and co-founder, Stephen Nelson-Smith, is internationally recognised as one of the foundational visionaries of the Devops movement, and a leading exponent of infrastructure-as-code and cloud computing. We have close relationships with many of the leading services companies spearheading the adoption of cloud-based and infrastructure-as-code strategies.

We have a history of happy clients and a business built primarily on referrals; a clear sign that we're getting it right for a lot of people.

In short

We specialise in bringing the best out of e-businesses through agile and lean coaching, building highly available and scalable architectures, and implementing advanced automation, configuration management and monitoring tools.
