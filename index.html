Agile Infrastructure Consultancy

With four years of successful problem-solving to our credit, our speciality is optimising web-based business infrastructure using innovative custom architectures, cutting-edge automation tools and best practice working methodologies.

We help businesses which rely on software to make money.

We love automation tools like Chef and Puppet, we love infrastructure-as-code, we love Agile, we love Kanban, we love Devops...

The one stop shop for solving hard problems.

We believe that the best way to help web-based businesses thrive is to enable them to build upon an infrastructure that has been designed and implemented as if it were code.

We help businesses find efficiency wins by introducing effective automation, monitoring and backup strategies, using Puppet and Chef.

We've been a part of the nascent Devops movement since its inception, and our principal consultant, Stephen Nelson-Smith, has written and presented widely on the subject. For more, see our blog.

We provide training, coaching and mentoring. We help people to implement good working practices inspired by devops.

We help our clients to get software from development to live in the most efficient and cost-effective way, and then once it's live, to keep it working well.

We're experts in custom infrastructure design, specialising in Python and Ruby web applications.
